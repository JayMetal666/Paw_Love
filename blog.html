Paw Love Blog Updates

by Paw Love March 16, 2023

At Paw Love Animal Rescue, we work to rescue and defend animals from harm everywhere. In addition to freeing and caring for various animals in Ireland, we also rescue wild animals from captivity in rough areas around dublin and sterilise and immunize stray dogs and cats in underdeveloped counties. Wherever it is possible, we put rescued animals back in their natural habitat, but we also give them a permanent home.
